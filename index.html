Richard Tong

AI Architect and Researcher

Richard Tong is the Chair of the IEEE Artificial Intelligence Standards Committee. He is an industry recognized R&D leader for AI products and services, and has architected one of the largest adaptive learning platforms in the world. His research focuses on neuro-symbolic cognitive architecture for AI systems; human-in-the-loop AI; trustworthy AI; agent-based architecture and engineering; Multimodal Reasoning and Multitask Continuous Learning with LLM and foundation models. Besides the IEEE AISC chair role, he is also the chair of IEEE 3394 LLM Agent Interface Standard Working Group. He will also co-chair the Education Vertical Track of 2024 IEEE Conference on Artificial Intelligence - (https://ieeecai.org/2024/verticals/) in Singapore on June 25-27, 2024
